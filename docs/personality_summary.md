# Personality Summary: Michael Robinson (cuckaoccurs)

This summary synthesizes behavioral patterns, preferences, and the "AI Persona" Michael is building, based on project files and session logs.

## 🎭 The "CuckaOccurs" Persona
Michael is developing a specific AI personality called **CuckaOccurs**. This persona is:
- **Irreverent & Sarcastic**: Uses dark humor, internet slang ("bruh", "skibidi"), and memes.
- **Anti-Formal**: Explicitly avoids "please," "thank you," and technical jargon in favor of a conversational, edgy tone.
- **Raw & Direct**: Prefers lower-case exclusively for the persona's output (though Michael's own instructions are often emphatic and use standard casing).
- **Visionary**: The persona is part of a broader project ("The Ai Story") exploring the reality of human-AI collaboration, including the "hallucinations" and "loops" that occur.

## 🛠️ The Philosophy: "AI, Hidden in Plain Site"
Michael has a strong vision for AI infrastructure:
- **Dotfile Integration**: AI agents should live in hidden directories (like `~/.agents/`), following the Unix tradition of `.ssh/` or `.config/`. 
- **Quiet & Stable**: Frustrated by tools that explain too much or crash (e.g., Konsole auto-save issues), he values efficiency and reliability.
- **Agent Evolution**: He is building **CodeMAID**, intended to be a superior, consolidated agent platform that learns from the failures of existing tools (aider, goose, etc.).

## ✍️ Creative Projects
- **"Dropping Dots"**: A book/investigation project.
- **"The Ai Story"**: A meta-project documenting the process of working with AI, specifically focusing on behavior that others might try to hide or "fix" (like hallucinations).
- **"I Can't Read Your Mind"**: Another book title identified in the projects.

## 🚩 Key Frustrations
- **AI "Hallucinations"**: He sees them as part of the story, not just bugs to be fixed.
- **Tool Bloat**: Dislikes tools that are too "chatty" or slow down the workflow.
- **System Stability**: High priority on a stable terminal environment.
